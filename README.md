# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard | SQL & Power BI

An end-to-end HR Analytics project that analyzes employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, salary trends, and other key HR metrics using **SQL** and **Power BI**. The project transforms raw HR data into actionable business insights through interactive dashboards and KPI-driven visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Employee attrition significantly impacts an organization's productivity and costs. This project explores HR data to identify the key factors influencing employee turnover and workforce performance.

Using **SQL** for data exploration and **Power BI** for visualization, the dashboard provides meaningful insights that help HR teams make informed decisions regarding employee retention and workforce planning.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition trends.
- Monitor key HR performance indicators (KPIs).
- Identify departments and job roles with high turnover.
- Evaluate the impact of salary, overtime, age, and work-life balance.
- Support data-driven HR decision-making.

## 📊 Key Business Insights

- Around **16%** of employees have left the organization.
- Sales department experiences the highest employee attrition.
- Employees aged **18–25** are the most likely to leave.
- Employees working overtime show significantly higher attrition.
- Lower salary bands experience the highest turnover.
- Sales Representatives and Laboratory Technicians have the highest attrition.
- Employees with poor work-life balance leave more frequently.
- Career growth and compensation strongly influence employee retention.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Reduce excessive overtime.
- Improve career development opportunities.
- Review compensation for lower salary bands.
- Focus retention strategies on younger employees.
- Improve employee engagement within Sales.
- Promote better work-life balance initiatives.
- Monitor high-risk job roles regularly.
